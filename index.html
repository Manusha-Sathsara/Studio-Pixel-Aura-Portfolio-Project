Taxila Astronomical Association Logo

LOGO DESIGN
2022 - March

Design Walkthrough

This logo was crafted to represent the Taxila Central College's Astronomical Association, embodying a sense of wonder and exploration. The bold "TAA" initials symbolize strength and unity, while the surrounding orbital ring evokes the vastness of space and the club’s commitment to astronomical discovery.

Meaning and Symbolism:

The orbit circling the initials signifies the club's journey into the cosmos, and the stylized planets add a touch of celestial charm. Each element was designed to balance professionalism with a hint of curiosity, reflecting the educational yet explorative nature of the club.

Outcome

The logo not only captures the essence of the Astronomical Association but also gives the students a visual identity that they can be proud of—one that connects their academic endeavors with their passion for space. The gold texture adds elegance, making it perfect for both digital and print use, ensuring a striking presence across all media

Studio PixelAura Logo Design

LOGO DESIGN

Meaning and Symbolism:

The torch stands as a beacon of creativity, illuminating the path of innovation. The rings symbolize the ripple effect of our designs, illustrating how each project goes beyond its initial scope, reaching and influencing broader audiences. Together, they encapsulate Studio PixelAura's purpose: to bring meaningful and lasting impact through the power of design.

Outcome

The final logo conveys sophistication and professionalism, perfectly suited for a studio that prides itself on quality and creative excellence. With its bold yet elegant design, it speaks to clients who value both artistry and meticulous craftsmanship, serving as an emblem of Studio PixelAura’s commitment to transformative, high-impact design.
